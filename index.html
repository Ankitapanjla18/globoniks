Why Traditional Coaching Fails

Traditional tech coaching often relies on spoon-feeding information through lectures, tutorials, and theoretical exercises. This approach creates passive learners who struggle to apply knowledge in real-world scenarios.

At GLOBONIKS, we believe in mentorship over coaching — a hands-on approach that develops critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and genuine expertise.
